# 🤖 DIY Robotics & Embedded Projects

Welcome to my personal collection of DIY robotics, embedded systems, and AI experiments. This repository is where I design, build, and test new ideas using microcontrollers, sensors, displays, and custom electronics.

## About

I enjoy creating interactive robots and smart devices that combine hardware and software. Most of my projects are built from scratch and focus on learning, experimentation, and open-source development.

My work includes:

* DIY Robots
* ESP32 and ESP32-C3 Projects
* Arduino-based Systems
* OLED and TFT Display Animations
* AI Assistants
* Sensor Integration
* Motion and Balance Systems
* Wireless Communication
* Computer Vision and AI Experiments
* Custom Electronic Prototypes

## Current Projects

### Project Rocky

A custom ESP32-based robotic companion featuring animated displays, sensors, and AI integration.

### OLED Visual Effects

Creative animations and physics simulations including:

* Motion tracking
* Particle systems
* Fluid and sand simulations
* Interactive graphics
* MPU6050-controlled effects

### AI Robot Assistant

A lightweight AI assistant powered by ESP32 hardware with external AI processing.

### Biped Robot

A two-servo balancing robot using motion sensors and real-time control algorithms.

### Embedded AI Experiments

Exploring local and remote AI models for robotics and human-machine interaction.
